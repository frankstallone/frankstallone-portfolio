Goku or Son Goku, is the main character of DragonBall. When we first meet him in DragonBall, he is a lone boy living on a mountain. Bulma, the second character we meet, tells him about the Dragonballs, which once all 7 are gathered, a single wish may be granted. But the strangest thing about Goku is that the youth has a tail. We soon learn that when Goku sees the full moon he transforms into a giant ape. He goes through DragonBall fighting against three main enimies: Pilaf, The Red Ribbon Army, and Piccolo Diamouh. During this period of time, Goku meets many people on his journeys but the most notable are Krillin, Tien, Chaozu, Yamcha, Puar, Oolong, Chi Chi, (who he marries in the future.) He learns the Kamehameha from Master Roshi, the first person he trains under. The Kamehameha is a basic energy blast which is the most used move in the entire show. Later he trains under Kami who helps him defeat Piccolo Diamouh. Then 3 years later, DragonBallZ begins. A reunion of the DragonBall characters is held, where we meet Gohan, Goku's son. The first enemy that we meet is Radditz, who tells Goku that he is a Sayian, a being from another planet, who eliminate all life from a planet and then sell it for a price to the highest bidder. Radditz tells him that there are only four Sayians left, including Goku and him, after their planet was destroyed. He then tells him that he was sent to Earth as a baby to destroy it, but Goku fell and hurt his head as a child, and lost his "programming." Radditz kidnaps Gohan and tells Goku to kill a hundred people or he will not give his son back. Goku refuses to do this, and teams up with Piccolo (Piccolo Diamouh's son) for a short while and together they defeat Radditz, but Goku dies in the process. As Radditz dies, he tells Piccolo that the other two Sayians will arrive in one year. Goku also learns this, so he crosses Snake Way, which takes him the nine months, to train with the great martial arts master King Kai. King Kai teaches him the Kaioken attack which increases your speed, strength, hearing, sight, and power. This can be done in different degrees like x2 or x3. Goku also learns the Spirit Bomb which is a gathering of the energy of the planet Goku is currently on. On King Kai's planet the Spirit Bomb is small because of the planet's incredibly small size, but if used on Earth, it will be much bigger. After his training is completed, Bulma and Master Roshi, who have gathered the Dragonballs, wish him back to life because the two Sayians, Nappa and Vegeta have arrived. The journey across Snake Way is much quicker this time, taking only a day. But when he arrives he finds that Yamcha, Choazu, Tien, and Piccolo have all been killed by the Sayian Nappa/Sabiamen. Gohan and Krillin are the only ones left living. He gives them each a half of a Senzu bean, and they are healed. He challenges Nappa, and easily defeats him, and in disgust, Vegeta kills his partner. Then there is gigantic battle between Vegeta and Goku, where Goku eventually wins with the help of Krillin,Gohan and even a little help from Yagierobe. In the end, where Krillin is in a position to kill Vegeta, Goku shows his compassion by telling Krillin to let Vegeta live. Krillin reluctantly watches as Vegeta leaves.
