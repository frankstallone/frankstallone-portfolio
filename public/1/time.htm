YEAR EVENT
More than 100 million years B.C. Earliest confirmation of existence of King Kaioh
Approx. 75 million years B.C. Somebody seals up the Kaiohshin Elder (15th generation Kaoihshin) in the Z-Sword
Approx. 5 million years B.C. The evil sorcerer Bibidi creates Majin Boo. In the following years, Boo destroys hundreds of planets. 4 of the five Kaoihshins are killed by Boo. Boo is sealed up in a sphere and taken to Earth by Bibidi. The Eastern Kaiohshin kills Bibidi and leaves Boo's sphere on the Earth's surface.
Tens of thousands of years B.C. Annin (Taijohroh-kun) begins her supervision over the Furnace of Eight Divinations, on the border between this world and the 'other side'.
4237 years B.C. The demons that are the ancestors of Garlic Jr. and the rest migrate from Makyoh-sei to Earth.
739 years B.C. Jadohshin (The God of Snake Way) becomes the Princess Snake and settles down in a house on the Snake Way.
About 238 years B.C. (According to Saiyan legend) The Super Saiyan from the Saiyan legend appears. The universe is shaken up by death and destruction
About 250 B.C. Uranai Baba begins fortunetelling.
261 - King Enma traverses Snake Way to get to King Kaioh. On the way, he meets the Princess Snake and she falls in love with him.

- An ecological disaster occurs on Planet Namek. Katattsu's son escapes. He makes an emergency landing on Earth.

430 A.D. Master Roshi is born.
431 Katattsu's son becomes the apprentice of Earth's King Kame.
448 Master Roshi(18 years old), under training by Mutaito, falls in love with Fan-Fan.
459 Tao-pie Pie is born.
461 - The demon Garlic assaults the Earth's King Kame, but is defeated.

- Katattsu's son becomes King Kame of Earth. 

- At that same time, the evil Piccolo Daimaoh is born out of the expelled evil from the new King Kame's body. He is sealed up in a rice cooker by Mutaito's Mafuba technique. Mutaito dies.

474 Dabla investigates Earth.

720-730 - On the Planet Plant, the Saiyans unite and start a rebellion. An all-out war breaks out between them and the Tsoofur-jin.

- The Tsoofur-jin are overrrun by the Saiyans. The Tsoofur scientist Dr. Lychee dies.

- The Hate-Amplifier 800 (Hacchihyakku) is shot into space.

- The Tsoofur-jin are utterly destroyed. The Saiyans conquer the entire planet, which is renamed Planet Vegeta. At the same time, they take over Tsoofur technology like the Scouter.

- Planet Vegeta gains control over planets Meat and Kanassa.

- King Vegeta's rebellion against Freeza ends as a failure.

- Freeza declares war to the Saiyans.

- Goku's father Bardock is killed in battle.

- Freeza destroys Planet Vegeta.

- Kakarot is sent away from Planet Vegeta. He lands on Earth.

- Ox King's daughter Chi Chi is born. Her mother dies of an illness.

737 between June and August Kakarot is found by the old Gohan. He calls the boy Goku.
738 Goku hits his head after a fall from a cliff. His personality changes completely and instead of a ferocious Saiyan he becomes a calm and cheerful boy.
739 Ox King and baby Chi Chi go for a picnic. During their absence, a fire spirit is unleashed in Mount Ryohkei near Ox King's castle, and the mountain starts erupting fire. After this, Mount Ryohkei's name changes to Mount Frypan. 
740 - Baby creates Dr. Myuu because he needs lots of energy. So he programs Myuu to create the Machine Mutants, which leads to the development of Ruudo and all of the other mutants.

- Oolong is born.

- Krillin begins his apprenticeship in the Oorin Temple.

749, month unknown In the cellar of her own house, Bulma discovers the 2-Star Dragonball.
749, 22 August In a cave in the north, Bulma discovers the 5-Star Dragonball.
