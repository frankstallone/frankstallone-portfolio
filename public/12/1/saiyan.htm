What is a Super Saiyan?


     
       
"Super Saiyan" is a legend passed down among the Saiyan, of a genius fighter among the Saiyan, who comes along only once in every thousand years. It is said that the Super Saiyan is cruel and powerful; so powerful, in fact, that there can be no one stronger in the universe. The Saiyan's hair turns gold, and the eyes turn bigger and green. The higher the Super Saiyan's power level is, the longer the hair grows. The highest Super Saiyan power level was 3; which Goku reached. There is a "Super Saiyan 4" in GT, but it is not a true "Super Saiyan" form because the hair remains black. It looks more like a half monkey, half Saiyan. Hair grows on the chest, a tail grows, eyes turn evil like.
   
     

Qualifications of becoming a Super Saiyan

     
   
  1 Must be at least partly Saiyan. 2
  2 Must possess at least enough power to potentially surpass the limits of a common Saiyan.
  3 Must be pure/have a pure heart.
  4 Extreme rage triggers the realization of being a Super Saiyan.
  5 After the first transformation into a Super Saiyan, the trans- formation becomes relatively easier each additional time.
     
     

How did Vegeta turn Super Saiyan?

     
 

Referring back to "Qualifications of becoming a Super Saiyan", Vegeta satisfies both the first and second qualifications easily. As for the third and fourth rule:

Vegeta: "I am pure EVIL!!"

(or "I do have a pure heart... It's just pure evil!!")

After seeing Trunks and Goku in Super Saiyan mode, Vegeta not only realizes the level of power of a Super Saiyan, but also the fact that there can be more than one Super Saiyan at one time. Vegeta's been trying to reach (and surpass) the power level of a Super Saiyan ever since. However, while he was training in the artificial gravity room (800x gravity), he realized that he has reached his limit, yet is still signifigantly weaker than a Super Saiyan.

Vegeta became enraged with himself, which in turns triggers his change into a Super Saiyan.
