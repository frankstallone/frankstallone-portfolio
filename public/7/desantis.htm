I like Shakespeare. His work seems to agree with me. I understand the way he writes, and after four years of reading his work, I've learned to appreciate it. Hamlet is no exception, the plot is quite entriguing. As true with all Shakespeare's work, the ending is always the final draw for all the main characters. I thought that the ending was executed extremely nicely. I was excited to find out how all of the main characters were going to perish. They all managed to die without any other outside involvement. The other main idea presented in Hamlet is the question of Hamlet's sanity. Hamlet is not crazy. Hamlet has personality traits that help define a psychotic person, but he is not crazy. Other than that, I really enjoyed the play.
