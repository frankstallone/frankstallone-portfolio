Well, I guess this is what you would call the creative side of English. While not writing papers or studying for tests we did our English project on Hamlet. The four of us had to find a unique and creative way to do a project on the "oh so famous" play of Hamlet so we decided that doing a web page would be the most creative and informative way. This site contains our opinions as well as a character analysis, pictures from the movie (the Mel Gibson version), and some of the Hamlet related sites that we came across while looking for information to use in our Hamlet research papers.
