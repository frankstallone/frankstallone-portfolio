Armor for Sleep - Things That Disappear
-March


Armor for Sleep, a band that has the instrumental sound of Thursday and lyrics which follow suit to that of Park have released their debut album entitled, "Things That Disappear." This album is all about struggles and loses. The band itself is rather young, almost all of them 18 years old, so with time they will mature and so will their music. For a debut album these guys have a head start on most of the competition. I haven't heard such a good debut album since Open Hand, and believe me when I tell you this CD will have something for everyone to relate to. The album will put you into a trance. I believe these guys have a lot of potential. I listen to it all the time, and enjoy the clean guitar and smooth sound of the vocals. Check it out!
